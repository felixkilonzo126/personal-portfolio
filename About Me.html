About Me

Hi, I’m Felix Kilonzo, a passionate and self-driven web developer based in Kenya. I specialize in building clean, responsive, and user-friendly websites that perform well across all devices.

With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP, I design websites that not only look good but function seamlessly. I also work with MySQL databases to ensure smooth backend operations, secure data handling, and dynamic content management.

One of my key strengths is creating responsive designs that adapt beautifully to smartphones, tablets, and desktops—because I believe every user deserves a great experience no matter what device they’re on.

As a student of software development, I’m continuously expanding my skills in database management, UI/UX best practices, and modern web technologies. My goal is to help individuals, small businesses, and organizations establish a strong and professional online presence.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy exploring new technological advancements and discovering Creative web ideas that inspire and improve my development work
