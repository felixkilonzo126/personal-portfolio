My Services

I am currently a student pursuing skills in software development, networking, and ICT services. I offer a focused range of services designed to help individuals, small businesses, and institutions achieve their digital goals. Whether you're starting out or need technical help, I’m here to provide affordable and efficient solutions.

Web Development

I create fully responsive and functional websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. Whether you need a personal blog, portfolio, or small business website, I can design and develop a site that’s fast, secure, and mobile-friendly. I also help with redesigning old websites to give them a modern look and feel. Hosting setup and domain connection are also part of the service if needed.

Networking Services

I provide basic to intermediate networking services such as setting up LAN connections, installing routers, troubleshooting network issues, and ensuring reliable internet access for homes and small offices. I also assist with printer sharing, file sharing, and IP configurations for smooth communication across devices. These services are ideal for cyber cafés, homes, and small businesses.

Database Management

I design, build, and manage databases using tools like MySQL and PHPMyAdmin. I help you organize, store, and retrieve data effectively—whether it’s for a school system, customer records, or employee information. I can also help integrate your database into a web system and ensure data is properly backed up and secure.

Cyber Café Services

I offer a variety of everyday ICT and cyber services including document typing, printing, scanning, photocopying, PDF conversions, photo printing, lamination, and internet access. I can also assist clients with basic online services such as email account creation, government portal access (eCitizen), HELB applications, and mobile banking services. These services are tailored for students, job seekers, and business people in need of quick tech help.
